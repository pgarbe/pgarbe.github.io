About Me

My name is Philipp Garbe and I work as Developer in Munich.

I started programming when I was a teen (some C++ but mostly VB). After .Net was first-time released I switched to C# and it is still my favourite language. But in the last couple of months I came more and more in contact with Ruby and F#. And not to forget my love-hate relationship with JavaScript. For almost all of my projects I use Git as version control and the more I work with it the more I love it.

Since several years now, I work for a great company in Munich. We work in very agile teams and I have the freedom to experiment with techniques like CQRS, Event Sourcing and DDD. I also believe that Continous Delivery (or Continous Deployment if possible) is fundamental for good teams and good products.

The thoughts and comments expressed in my blog posts are my own and not my employer.
